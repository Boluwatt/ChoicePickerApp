Choice Picker

Picking a random choice could be really stressful, tasking and time consuming so we decided to help you with that.

The choice could be as simple as a choice between eating bread or rice or as difficult as choosing whether or not to post a picture.

P.S You can only have 2 choices at least and 5 choices at most. Your input should be less than 30 characters.
